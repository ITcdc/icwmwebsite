Our Objectives

  • Dissemination and regular updating of knowledge base on Waste Management and its diffusion among our members through various events and knowledge sharing platforms as a resource and knowledge management centre.
  • Developing an independent, robust and transparent regulatory mechanism that is dynamic and can evolve with needs and demands of the changing times.
  • Acting as an accreditation body in the field of sustainability development and waste management courses by adhering to the highest ethical standards.
  • Developing a stream of professionally qualified, well trained and skilled human resources in the waste management sector.
  • Exploring new avenues in sustainable waste management through proper planning and extensive Research and Development.
  • Highlighting current issues in waste management sector and showcasing emerging technologies and best practices through proper documentation and well managed database.
  • Extending our reach in the society by joining hands with individuals and organizations involved in waste management or aspiring to be involved in the sector.
  • Inculcating social objectives of this profession by community involvement and constant capacity building as well as knowledge enhancement.
  • Acting as a qualifying body for quality waste management and conducting regular examinations for constant quality improvement of our members.
  • Providing a common platform where all our stakeholders can share their knowledge and experiences; discuss their concerns and issues and together come up with innovative and appropriate solutions for a sustainable waste management.
