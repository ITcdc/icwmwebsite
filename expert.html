Dr. Vivek Agarwal

Dr. Agarwal has been working in the field of waste management for over two decades now. He holds MBBS from the Sawai Man Singh Medical College, and a degree in MBA and BJMC. He is an active part of the International Solid Waste Association, and Chair of the working group on Collection and Transportation Technology. Dr. Agarwal has also lent his name to numerous associations in the field of waste management, and has been an architect of developing social NGOs addressing global causes.

Annakshetra Foundation and Center for Development Communication are just a couple of initiatives among the several others that have taken the shade under the larger umbrella of CDC Group. With his experience Dr. Agarwal brings an additional insight into the field of waste management in India, while giving us a vision of the future of efficient waste management techniques around the world. As the Managing Director of Greentech Infra Pvt. Ltd., Dr. Agarwal is providing guidance to the team of expert waste managers who are thriving to provide customized waste management solutions to the global community.

Prof. Neeraj Gupta

Professor Neeraj Gupta is an Architect, Planner and Management Consultant, having 30 Years of Experience in Urban Planning and Management including about 15 years in Capacity building for Urban Environmental Services. Presently, he is Dean of School of Architecture and Head of the Department of Architecture, Central University of Rajasthan.

He is advisor to Indian Institute of Science Education and Research – Kolkata, Indian Institute of Management Udaipur, Indian Institute of Technology Rajasthan, Jodhpur as member of Campus Development Project Management Group, Centre for Development Communication and Kanak Resources Management Limited (ILFS’s subsidiary company) for Training and Human Resource Development and Community Interventions in Municipal Waste Management, Sanitation and Health in urban areas.

He has demonstrable experience of establishing new and innovative teaching and training courses and academic programmes leading to UG and PG degrees. He has designed and implemented more than 150 thematic training courses over a period of nine years in Rajasthan State Institute of Public Administration. This included Management Development Programmes for executives of Public and Private Sector Organizations, Specialized courses for Engineers on Project Management, Environmental Planning, Project Finance; Course for Senior Level Administrators on issues related to Good Governance, Privatization, Development Management etc.

Prof. A K Ojha

Former member of Rajasthan Administrative Services, Prof. A.K.Ojha has a post graduate degree in English Literature from the University of Rajasthan. In his year long career, he held important posts in district administration, Urban Improvement Trust and Municipal Boards/Councils. He also served as Deputy Secretary, Urban Development and Housing, Deputy Secretary, Chief Minister of Rajasthan and member of Rajasthan Tax Board.

He is well travelled and was chosen by the govt. of Rajasthan to work on Gender issues under Gender Planning Training project at University of Sussex, UK. After superannuation from the government, he became visiting professor of good governance and also remained member secretary, Centre for Good Governance, Rajasthan and State Institute of Public Administration, Jaipur.

Shantanu Srivastava

Shantanu brings over sixteen years of experience in strategic planning management consulting, marketing & branding, and sales & business development. Shantanu has worked at leading financial institutions and has extensive domain expertise in the secured lending business, structured finance and asset management. At HDFC bank Shantanu was responsible for sales and business development for its mortgage business in north India where he led the aggressive campaign to achieve the bank’s position as “market leader” in the region in the mortgages market.

Prior to HDFC bank, Shantanu was Mortgage Business Head, Rajasthan at Citigroup where he scaled up the Citi’s mortgage business in the state to attain market leadership. At Citigroup he was responsible for Business Development, Profit center management & delinquency management. Most recently, as a management consultant, Shantanu has helped C-level executives build corporate strategies, support inorganic business growth and implement change management to drive organizational performance. Shantanu holds an MBA in Marketing & HR from University of Rajasthan and a degree in Journalism and Mass Communications from Centre for Mass communication, Jaipur.
