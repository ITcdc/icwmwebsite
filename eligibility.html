Eligibility for Membership

* Public Policy, Social Policy, International development, Environmental Science, Engineering & Management and Public Health Program students at Masters and PhD level (full and part time enrolled students in various Government and Private Universities across India and abroad). * Faculty involved with waste management studies (from areas of Public Policy, Social Policy, International development, Environmental Sciences, Environmental Engineering, Civil Engineering, Chemical Engineering, Life Sciences, and Public Health etc.) * Industrial sectors involved with waste management and waste handling at all levels (Textile Manufacturing and Processing Industries, Leather Industries, Battery Manufacturers, Tile Manufacturers, Cement Plants, Hospitality Industry, Construction Sector, Electronics and Electrical goods Manufacturers, etc.) * Municipal bodies (Municipal Corporations, Municipal Councils, ULBs, etc.) * Private organizations involved with waste management (Working in waste management sector on PPP mode) * Non-Government Organizations involved in environmental and waste management sector. * Individuals involved with waste management sector at operational level for more than one year (from Government Sector, Private Sector, Industries, Academics, etc.)
